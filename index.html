Introduction of F1

Formula 1 (F1) is top-tier open-wheel car racing known for its high-speed, technologically advanced cars, governed by the FIA. Teams with two drivers compete in a series of races on diverse global circuits, accumulating points for championship contention. The sport blends cutting-edge engineering, aerodynamics, and hybrid power units, captivating a massive worldwide audience.
